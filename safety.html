General Safety Guidelines

To ensure that every visitor enjoys a safe and pleasant experience, please follow the general safety guidelines. Be mindful of your surroundings, respect other visitors, and help us keep Camperdown Memorial Rest Park a welcoming space for all.

Legal Requirements

All visitors are required to comply with local laws and posted restrictions to help preserve the park’s legacy and beauty for future generations.

As you capture the beauty of Camperdown Memorial Rest Park, please remember to respect others. Avoid disturbing people as you take photos, and keep noise to a minimum. If you’re photographing pets or people, kindly ask for their permission first to ensure everyone’s comfort and privacy.

Protect the Park

Help us keep Camperdown Memorial Rest Park clean and enjoyable for everyone. Dispose of rubbish in designated bins, and be mindful of maintaining the park’s natural environment.
Let’s work together to preserve this space as a sanitary and welcoming area for all visitors.
